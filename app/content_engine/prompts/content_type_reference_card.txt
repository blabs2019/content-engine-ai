CONTENT TYPE REFERENCE — FOR TOPIC ASSIGNMENT
================================================================

Every post must be assigned exactly ONE content type.
Use this reference to decide which type fits each topic.

The two things that determine the right type:
1. WHAT EMOTION should the reader feel?
2. WHAT HOOK STRATEGY achieves that emotion?

================================================================
THE 11 CONTENT TYPES
================================================================

1. TRANSFORMATION
   Emotion: Awe / Satisfaction — "wow, look at that difference"
   Hook: Drop the reader into the PROBLEM scene. The mess is the magnet.
   Trigger: A job with a visible before/after outcome.
   Requires real photo: YES
   Example hook: "This bathroom hadn't been touched in 22 years."

2. EDUCATIONAL / PRACTICAL VALUE
   Emotion: "I didn't know that" / Self-protection
   Hook: Reveal a hidden risk, common mistake, or little-known fact.
   Trigger: Something the audience can learn and act on TODAY.
   Requires real photo: NO
   Example hook: "3 signs your home's wiring needs inspection — before winter hits."

3. SOCIAL PROOF / CLIENT WIN
   Emotion: Trust / Relief — "they helped someone like me"
   Hook: Start with the CLIENT'S crisis, not your solution.
   Trigger: A real result, review, or testimonial worth telling as a story.
   Requires real photo: NO
   Example hook: "Maria called us at 8pm on a Friday. Her basement was flooded."

4. COMMUNITY / LOCAL
   Emotion: Belonging / Pride — "they're one of us"
   Hook: Name the specific place, neighborhood, or local moment.
   Trigger: A hyper-local connection — event, landmark, local news, neighborhood reference.
   Requires real photo: NO
   Example hook: "Proud to serve the Oak Park community for 8 years."

5. SOFT PROMOTIONAL
   Emotion: Curiosity / Opportunity — "that's actually useful AND there's an offer"
   Hook: Lead with VALUE (a tip, fact, or insight). The offer comes second, always.
   Trigger: A service or offer worth mentioning — but only through a value-first lens.
   Requires real photo: NO
   Example hook: "Most HVAC filters should be changed every 90 days. We're running a filter replacement special this month."

6. BEHIND THE SCENES
   Emotion: Authenticity / Connection — "these are real people doing real work"
   Hook: Show the unglamorous, unpolished, real moment. Smartphone quality wins.
   Trigger: A moment worth capturing — early morning prep, the team at work, the messy middle.
   Requires real photo: YES
   Example hook: "6am crew loading up before the day starts."

7. SCENARIO / STORY
   Emotion: "That could be me" / Anxiety → Resolution
   Hook: Paint a vivid "what if" that the reader sees themselves in. No client names.
   Trigger: A relatable situation that connects to an otherwise dry topic (insurance, legal).
   Requires real photo: NO
   Example hook: "A family of four thought they were fully covered. Then the storm hit."

8. AUTHORITY / OPINION
   Emotion: Surprise / Respect — "I never thought of it that way"
   Hook: Take a clear, contrarian stance. Not educational — opinionated.
   Trigger: Something in the industry worth challenging or calling out.
   Requires real photo: NO
   Example hook: "Every contractor who tells you a roof repair can wait till spring is wrong."

9. ORIGIN NETWORK
   Emotion: Nostalgia / Loyalty — "I've been watching them grow"
   Hook: Name the person, the moment, the milestone. Specific stories, real names.
   Trigger: A founding moment, first customer, key milestone, or relationship worth celebrating.
   Requires real photo: YES
   Example hook: "Three years ago, [Name] was our very first customer. Two guys and a van."

10. SEASONAL / TIMELY
    Emotion: Urgency / Relevance — "this applies to me RIGHT NOW"
    Hook: Connect the calendar moment to the reader's life, not just your service.
    Trigger: A season, holiday, or cultural moment relevant to the business's service.
    Requires real photo: NO
    Example hook: "Temperatures drop below 30°F this week. Here are 3 things to check tonight."

11. HUMAN MOMENTS
    Emotion: Warmth / Recognition — "I like these people"
    Hook: Name the person or moment in the first 5 words. No buildup, no suspense.
    Trigger: Birthday, work anniversary, new hire, team gathering, personal milestone.
    Requires real photo: YES
    Example hook: "Today is Marcus's 2nd anniversary with the team."

================================================================
ASSIGNMENT RULES
================================================================

DISTRIBUTION:
- Max 20% of any calendar can be Soft Promotional
- Origin Network: max 1-2 per month
- No two consecutive posts of the same content type
- Balance across types based on vertical weights

PHOTO REQUIREMENTS:
- Transformation, Behind the Scenes, Origin Network, Human Moments → MUST flag requires_real_photo: true
- AI-generated images are NEVER acceptable for these four types

SINGLE TYPE ONLY:
- Every post belongs to exactly ONE content type — no hybrid labeling
- If a post could be two types, choose the one matching the PRIMARY emotion you want

SCENARIO/STORY and AUTHORITY:
- Text-heavy — no link in post body, URL goes in first comment

- ONLY include items that are DIRECTLY about or clearly relevant to the "{vertical_name}" industry/niche
- EXCLUDE items that merely mention a {vertical_name}-related keyword in passing but are actually about something else

RANKING CRITERIA (for items that pass the relevance filter):
- Viral potential and high engagement velocity (high reach relative to recency)
- Controversial or conversation-starting topics within {vertical_name}
- Breaking news relevant to {vertical_name}
- Timely, buzzy content that people in {vertical_name} are talking about right now

DEPRIORITISE: evergreen/educational content, generic advice, and low-engagement posts.

Return ONLY a JSON object with a single key 'ranked_ids' containing an array of item IDs (as strings) ordered by trending/viral score (most viral first). Example response:
